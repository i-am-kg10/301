Education!

Over the years I have been lucky enough to attend a wide variety of academic institutions. During my childhood I was enrolled in Stoneybrooke Christian Schools a small school located in Ladara Ranch, California. Later, I attended Sage Hill School, a newly formed private school in Newport Beach, where I had the opportunity to learn and grow with the school.

Currently I am on track to earn a Dual Degree from USC, a Bachlor of Science in Economomics and in Mathematics, as well as a Bachlor of Arts in Political Science and a Web Development minor. I hope to use these degrees to help pave my way towards a career in Finance, eventually Wall Street.For my freshman year of college I moved Washington DC where I attended George Washington University. Living in DC was an incredible experience, however due to family circumstances I had to move back to Californian and thus I transferred to USC.

The Trojan Family University of Southern California
